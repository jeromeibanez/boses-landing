FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Boses?

Boses is an AI-powered market research platform that simulates how real Southeast Asian consumers react to your product, campaign, or concept. It uses AI personas built for Southeast Asia to deliver consumer insights in hours — not months.

How accurate are Boses AI personas vs. real consumers?

Boses runs ongoing validation studies comparing AI persona output against real human in-depth interview data. The methodology takes a real-world IDI study, generates matched AI persona responses to the same script, then scores theme-overlap against the human baseline. Methodology and re-run instructions live inside the platform's validation suite.

How is Boses different from traditional market research?

Traditional market research in Southeast Asia is expensive ($20,000+ per study) and slow (3–6 months to results), so most teams can only afford to run it once a year. Boses delivers the same depth of insight in under an hour at a fraction of the cost, using AI personas built for SEA markets.

What markets does Boses support?

Boses is purpose-built for Southeast Asia. It supports consumer simulation across the Philippines, Indonesia, Vietnam, Thailand, Malaysia, and Singapore — with culturally accurate personas for each market that reflect real local values, behaviours, and purchase drivers.

How long does a Boses simulation take?

Most simulations complete in under an hour, depending on the size of your panel and the simulation type. What used to take 3–6 months of recruiting, facilitation, and analysis now happens before your next meeting.

What types of research can I run on Boses?

Boses supports eight simulation types on a single platform: Concept Test, In-Depth Interview (IDI, with AI-assisted and live-chat modes), Survey, Focus Group, Conjoint / Trade-Off Analysis, Comparative Test, Price Sensitivity Meter (PSM / Van Westendorp), and Customer Journey Mapping (CJM). You can mix and match methods within the same project.

How are Boses personas created?

Boses offers two persona types: AI-generated personas built from your target market parameters (age range, location, occupation, income level), and Boses-curated personas — a pre-built panel covering Southeast Asia's most common consumer segments. Both types produce realistic, culturally accurate responses grounded in local consumer reality.

Can I use Boses simulation results in investor pitches or board decks?

Yes. Boses outputs include shareable public report links (no login required), full transcripts, and methodology documentation. Several customers cite simulation findings in fundraising materials and board reviews.

How does Boses handle data privacy?

All persona data is synthetic — no real consumer PII is stored. Customer briefings are encrypted at rest, scoped to the uploading organization, and never used to train upstream language models.
